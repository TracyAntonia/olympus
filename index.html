Home

Welcome to Olympus Fitness Center, a gym with a difference. The gym has qualified gym instructors and all types of training equipments needed to become the best you. They include a wide variety of handpicked equipments like cardio machines, toning and heavy-wight dumbells, cross cable machines for multiple exercises, punching bags, targets and more.
Our main goal in this field of fitness is to help you see the big picture of your well-being and help you achieve your dream body. We deal with all kinds of people; those who want to lose weight, gain weight, those with chronic illness and also self defense training.
If you're looking for a comfortable, friendly, supportive, challenging and an affordable place to exercise, then Olympus Fitness Center is the right place to be.

Our Services

Physiotherapy

It helps to restore movement and functionality when one is affected by an injury or illness. It helps reduce risk of injury or illness in the future. Our sessions are strictly professional and they extend to be offered at the comfort of your home.

Fitness

It's a wide area as it consists of strength training, cardiovascular training(cardio) and nutrition. We always ensure that our clients are good in all the three areas,fit as one would call it, as it improves one's mood and physique.
An hour's workout is just 4% of your day.

Boxing

It's a fantastic way to improove functional strength for both women and men. The different techniques used in training help to build muscular endurance and longevity which increase strength and physical ability that carries thhrough to everyday life outside of the gym.
